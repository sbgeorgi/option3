Smart Architecture

An engine that actually gets your vibe.

Most travel apps just dump the top 10 tourist traps on you. We built an Advanced Recommendation Engine that puts you in control of your vacation planning.

Rank your top categories, select your hyper-specific sub-vibes (like Boutique Hotels or Dive Bars), and the NomadSwipe algorithm mathematically weights your deck. You only see what you actually care about.

The NomadSwipe Flow

Discovery without the drag.

Swipe Discovery

No endless scrolling. Right to save, left to pass. We compress hours of travel blog reading into a fast, highly-curated visual deck.

Interactive Routing

Every swipe right automatically plots onto your personal trip map. Instantly see how your saves cluster geographically to plan your days.

Group Sync

Invite the group chat. Everyone swipes on their own time. The map highlights mutual matches. Group consensus, zero arguments.
