Smart Architecture

An engine that actually gets your vibe.

Most travel apps just dump the top 10 tourist traps on you. We built an Advanced Recommendation Engine that puts you in control of your vacation planning.

Rank your top categories, select your hyper-specific sub-vibes (like Boutique Hotels or Dive Bars), and the NomadSwipe algorithm mathematically weights your deck. You only see what you actually care about.

The NomadSwipe Flow

Discovery without the drag.

Swipe Discovery

No endless scrolling. Right to save, left to pass. We compress hours of travel blog reading into a fast, highly-curated visual deck.

Interactive Routing

Every swipe right automatically plots onto your personal trip map. Instantly see how your saves cluster geographically to plan your days.

Group Sync

Invite the group chat. Everyone swipes on their own time. The map highlights mutual matches. Group consensus, zero arguments.

NomadSwipe FAQ

Short answers for travelers deciding whether NomadSwipe fits the way they plan.

What is NomadSwipe?

NomadSwipe is a swipe-based travel planning app that helps you discover restaurants, attractions, hotels, and nightlife based on your preferences.

Who is NomadSwipe for?

NomadSwipe is for travelers and groups who want to make trip decisions faster without juggling tabs, spreadsheets, and group chat debates.

Where is NomadSwipe available?

NomadSwipe is available now on Google Play for Android. The page marks the iOS App Store release as coming soon.

How does the recommendation engine work?

You rank categories and choose specific sub-vibes, then NomadSwipe weights your swipe deck toward the places you care about most.

Where can I review NomadSwipe privacy information?

The NomadSwipe Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is processed, how it may be shared, and how to exercise privacy rights.
