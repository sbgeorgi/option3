Request deletion

How to request deletion

  1. Email privacy@nomadswipe.com from the email address associated with your NomadSwipe account, if possible.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my NomadSwipe account".
  3. Include enough information for NomadSwipe to identify the account or app data connected to your request.

What deletion covers

A deletion request may cover account information and personal information associated with NomadSwipe, subject to identity verification, legal obligations, security requirements, and retention rules described in the Privacy Policy.

Consent and privacy choices

For access, correction, deletion, or consent withdrawal requests, use the contact path above or the request methods listed in the Privacy Policy.
